About Us

We are trying to make a difference by taking kids out of school that are disrupting the class and put them in schools that don't care if they disrupt the class and don't get consequences for their actions.

This may sound strange but studies have shown that disruptive kids work better in disruptive environments and quiet kids work better in quiet environments.

Our project was also named after an infamous case where a child, named Bob, would not stop being disruptive in class.
